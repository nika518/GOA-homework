Eat whole foods: Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Hydrate well: Aim for at least 8 cups (2 liters) of water daily, more if you're active. Limit processed foods: Avoid sugary snacks, fast food, and highly processed meals when possible. Mindful eating: Eat slowly, listen to hunger/fullness cues, and avoid distractions during meals.


Physical Activity Move daily: Try to get at least 30 minutes of moderate activity (walking, cycling, dancing, etc.). Mix it up: Combine cardio, strength training, and flexibility exercises for overall fitness. Stay consistent: Routine is key—find activities you actually enjoy. Mental & Emotional Well-being

Reduces stress and anxiety: Exercise, sleep, and mindfulness all help regulate stress hormones. Improves mood: Healthy foods and regular movement boost serotonin and endorphins—your feel-good chemicals. Better focus and memory: A healthy brain runs on proper nutrition, hydration, and rest.
